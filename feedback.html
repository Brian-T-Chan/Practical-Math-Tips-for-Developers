This site is intended to provide practical tips for people who need help with math at various levels (ranging from high school to university level). It is also intended for those who prefer an alternative to reading a textbook or attending a lecture. The content is geared towards developers as concepts and examples are illustrated using JS code snippets. Feedback is welcome (see the feedback page which includes my feedback email).

Current Content:
Order of operations and related notions (Algebra). How are vectors different from points (Geometry). Compute distances (Geometry). Variable substitutions for formulas and equations (Variables). How to add and multiply matrices (Matrices). Visual explanation of how binary numbers work (Binary Numbers).
